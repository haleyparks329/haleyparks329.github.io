## What changed

The homepage started using a desk metaphor because the work is easier to understand as objects in progress than as a polished marketing funnel.

## Why

A tidy portfolio can make every project feel equally finished. That was not honest for this site. The projects needed room for notes, boundaries, demos, unfinished questions, and public artifacts.

## What I noticed

A good public work surface can feel like someone stepped away for a minute and left the useful artifacts where they can be inspected.

## Next

Keep the homepage as the discovery layer, while project pages, writing, and Field Log entries carry the more precise explanations.

## Boundary

The metaphor should support the content. It should not become decoration that makes the actual work harder to read.
