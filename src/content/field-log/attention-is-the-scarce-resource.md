## What changed

This note named the design pressure behind much of the work on the site: useful systems should reduce the amount a person has to hold in their head without hiding uncertainty or removing judgment.

## Why

The public projects kept returning to the same pattern. The work was not only about making another interface. It was about memory, coordination, evidence, and context.

## What I noticed

Attention is the scarce resource. The useful role for software is often to remember, connect, search, and coordinate so the person can spend less effort holding the system together.

## Next

Keep using this as a design test for the projects: does the system give attention back, or does it create another place that needs to be managed?

## Boundary

This is still a working principle, not a complete theory of personal AI.
